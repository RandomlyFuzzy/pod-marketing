## Platforms: Etsy & eBay | POD Fulfillment: Printify, CustomCat, SPOD, Printful, Gelato
### Generated: May 27, 2026

## Executive Summary

Summer POD on Etsy and eBay remains highly profitable in 2026. **Etsy supports 36-56% net margins** driven by personalization and occasion-based buying. **eBay moves higher volume at 24-43% margins** but with tighter price sensitivity. **CustomCat and SPOD** offer faster turnaround than Printify (2-3 day production vs Printify's variable 2-7 days). **T-shirts, tote bags, and hats** are the anchor products. **Posters and stickers** offer the highest percentage margins; **canvas prints and hoodies** deliver the most dollar profit per sale.

**Fastest providers vs Printify:**
- **SPOD** — 48-hour production SLA, 4-7 day total delivery. Beats Printify's best providers and is far more consistent.
- **CustomCat** — 2-3 day production, single Michigan facility. Excellent for US-focused sellers.
- **Gelato** — Faster than Printify internationally due to local production in 32 countries.
- **Why they're faster:** CustomCat, SPOD, and Printful own their production or use tightly vetted partner networks. Printify is a marketplace of 90+ independent providers — speed varies wildly by which provider you pick.

## 10. Recommended Provider Strategy

| If You Care About | Use | Why |
|-------------------|-----|-----|
| **Lowest base cost** | Printify (posters, canvas, stickers) + CustomCat (tees, mugs, cases) | Mix providers per product |
| **Fastest delivery** | SPOD (48hr SLA) or CustomCat (2-3 day production) | 4-8 day total vs Printify's 4-12+ |
| **Best quality consistency** | Printful | In-house, strictest QA |
| **International sales** | Gelato | 32-country local production |

## 12. Key Takeaways

1. **Etsy outperforms eBay for summer POD profitability** — margins of 32-50% vs eBay's 11-36%, driven by personalization and occasion-based buying. A personalized family tee on Etsy nets $10.12 on Printify vs $1.71 on eBay.

2. **No single provider is cheapest across all products.** Use CustomCat for tees/mugs/cases/hats ($7.95 B+C 3001) and Printify for posters/canvas ($5.02 poster). This mix gives the lowest overall costs.

3. **SPOD and CustomCat beat Printify on speed** — 2-3 day production vs Printify's highly variable 2-7 days. For summer (tight event windows), speed matters. SPOD's 48-hour SLA is the industry best.

4. **Personalization is the profit lever** — adding names/dates/years bumps Etsy retail price $5-10 with zero additional production cost, boosting margins 5-10 points. 81% of consumers prefer personalized products (ZIK Analytics POD statistics, 2026).

5. **The Vacation Mom is your primary customer** — she represents 35-40% of summer POD volume, orders 4-6 items at $100-180, and has low price sensitivity because the vacation is already paid for.

6. **Non-apparel beats apparel for margin** — posters (48%), mugs (42%), and phone cases (44%) consistently outperform t-shirts in margin percentage. Bundle them as add-ons to apparel orders.

7. **Act now for July peak** — the 30-60 day SEO indexing window means late May is the last viable launch window for July 4th and peak summer vacation buying.
